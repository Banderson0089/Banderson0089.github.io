Vivid Product
Informational Document

Process

I created a "Fact Sheet" informational report on Maxar's Vivid Product line to streamline the process of reporting statistics for this product. Using a custom-built Python ArcModel script, the process automatically extracts relevant data points from the imagery metadata shapefile, performs the necessary calculations, and generates a PDF containing all the essential information for our end consumers. This automation significantly enhances efficiency and ensures accuracy in the reporting process.
